OVER POPULATION

Overpopulation is one of the most pressing issues in the Philippines. The Philippines' population crisis is getting worse every year. The condition of being populated with an excessively large number of people, according to the dictionary, is known as overpopulation. Overpopulation is one of the most serious environmental and social problems because it causes global warming, pollution, hunger, the sixth mass extinction, habitat loss, unemployment, and other problems.
